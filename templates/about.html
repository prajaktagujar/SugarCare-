Causes of Diabetes

  • Genetic factors
  • Poor diet & lack of exercise
  • Obesity
  • High blood pressure & cholesterol
  • Hormonal imbalances

Role of Age in Diabetes

As we age, the risk of developing Type 2 Diabetes increases. This is due to slower metabolism, weight gain, and reduced insulin sensitivity.

How to Prevent Diabetes?

  • Maintain a balanced diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Keep your weight in check
  • Monitor blood sugar levels
  • Reduce stress and maintain a healthy lifestyle

Precautions to Take

If you are at risk of diabetes, follow these precautions:

  • Eat fiber-rich and low-sugar foods
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Avoid processed and junk food
  • Regular health check-ups
  • Stay physically active
